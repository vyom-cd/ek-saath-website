The story

Coffee, pizza,
and a room full of regulars.

Ek Saath started in Bhilai with one idea — a place where families, students, and coffee people could all feel at home. We pull from multiple roasters because no single bean tells the whole story, and we stretch our own pizza dough because it’s worth the time.

We’re pure vegetarian. Always. And we’re open until midnight because the best conversations start late.
